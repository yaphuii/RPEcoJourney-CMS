About RPEcoJourney

RPEcoJourney is a web-based Content Management System designed to promote sustainability awareness through visually compelling multimedia content. Our mission is to educate and inspire users about environmental, social, and governance (ESG) topics through engaging and accessible content.

Vision: Create a community of informed individuals committed to sustainable practices and positive environmental impact.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What file formats are supported?

Images: PNG, JPG, GIF | Videos: MP4, WebM | Documents: PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT

What is the maximum file size for uploads?

Images: 10MB | Videos: 500MB | Documents: 50MB

How long does it take for published content to appear?

Content is published immediately after submission. It will be visible in the Browse section and available to all users right away.

Can I edit or delete content after publishing?

Published content cannot be edited directly. If you need to make changes, you can delete the old content and create a new version with updates.

How do I find my published content?

Your published content appears in your Content Producer dashboard. You can also find it by browsing the content library using category filters.

What are tags and how should I use them?

Tags are keywords that help users find your content. Use relevant, descriptive tags separated by commas (e.g., "renewable energy, climate change, solar power").

How do I change my account password?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Account Settings, scroll to "Change Password", enter your current password, create a new password, and click "Save Changes".

Can I have multiple user accounts?

Each user is limited to one account. Contact support if you need to manage multiple roles or accounts.
